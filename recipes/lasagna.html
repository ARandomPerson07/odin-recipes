World's Best Lasagna

Credits to John Chandler on Allrecipes for the recipe. And Dotdash Meredith Food Studios for the image.

This webpage was made as part of a milestone project on The Odin Project and is purely for demonstrative purposes.

This lasagna requires a little bit of work, but it's so worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat (sweet Italian sausage works best)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Products
    • Crushed tomatoes, one can
    • Tomato sauce, two cans
    • Tomato paste, two cans
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
    • Parsley, fresh
    • Basil, dried
    • Salt and pepper
    • Italian seasoning
    • Fennel Seeds
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions below.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
