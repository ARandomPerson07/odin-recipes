Cheesy Instant Ramen

Credits to Emily on Allrecipes for the recipe. And Dotdash Meredith Food Studios for the image.

This webpage was made as part of a milestone project on The Odin Project and is purely for demonstrative purposes.

Too lazy to make dinner? This easy meal will hold you over: I'm talking to you, college students!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Instant Ramen Pack (any flavour, 3oz.)
  • American Cheese Slice, 1 pc.

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add ramen noodles and boil until soft.
  3. Pour out the water. Add cheese and flavour packet. Mix until homogeneous.
